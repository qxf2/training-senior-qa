We expect this exercise to take you somewhere between 15-minutes to 2 hours. If you take more than two hours, please post what you tried along wtih error messages to our Skpye Channel.

ChromeDriver is available for Chrome on Android and Chrome on Desktop (Mac, Linux, Windows and ChromeOS).We can install the latest version according to the OS.

In keeping with our attempt to help you think in different ways, we have listed three approaches that work. There is no shame or pride in choosing anyone of them. Just choose the method you are comfortable with and proceed.

 

Approach 1: Try it out yourself

  1. We'd like to promote independent thinking and problem solving. So if you feel ready, try to install Chrome Driver without referring to any documentation, go for it!

 

Approach 2: Google for instructions

  1. Google for Chrome Driver install 'your operating system name'
  2. Follow the instructions

 

Approach 3: follow instructions

  1. Visit https://chromedriver.chromium.org/downloads
  2. Click on the latest 84.x release
  3. On the index page, choose chromedriver for your operating system
  4. Download the executable and follow the steps

 

Exercise

Once you are done, add the chromedriver executable to your path environment variable. Hint: Google for 'add chromedriver to path environment variable your operating system name'.

 

Verify your setup

After you are done with the exercise listed above, it is time to verify your ChromeDriver installation. It is a good habit to take small steps and verify them immediately. This will help you avoid getting stuck later on. To verify your setup, pull up your terminal prompt and type chromedriver. You should see the version you installed along with message as "starting.."
