What to expect

At the end of this mini-tutorial, you can expect to have setup Git Bash on your machine.

Most tutorials and training material expect things to go smoothly. Not us! We are experienced professional QA and we know that a lot can go wrong during setup. And many times it is not the learner's fault. So before we begin, here is what to expect as you go through the tutorial.

  1. Beginners usually have trouble with setup. That's ok. There is help.
  2. Over time, you will develop a mental catalogue of problems that come with install and setup.

When you hit an issue

When you run into an issue, don't panic. Here is what you can try:

  1. Take a minute to try and read through the error message. It's ok if seems like gibberish ... just make the attempt.
  2. Try and identify the key line in the error. If you cannot, reach out to us on Skype.
  3. Google for the phrase 'Git Bash install key line in error' e.g.: 'git bash install windows Permission Denied Error'
  4. Try out the suggestions mentioned in the Google search results even if you don't fully understand them.
  5. After you have tried your best, contact Qxf2 on our public Skpye Channel
